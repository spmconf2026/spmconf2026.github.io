Visa

Participants coming to Türkiye may require a visa depending on their nationality. Türkiye offers an e-Visa application system for many countries, which is fast and easy to use. We recommend checking the official Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Republic of Türkiye for up-to-date visa requirements.

Explore Istanbul

Istanbul offers a unique blend of East and West, ancient and modern. While attending the conference, take time to explore the city's rich history, vibrant streets, and stunning architecture.

  • Hagia Sophia – A UNESCO World Heritage Site and architectural wonder.
  • Topkapi Palace – The former palace of Ottoman sultans.
  • Blue Mosque – Famous for its six minarets and blue tiles.
  • Grand Bazaar – One of the world’s oldest and largest covered markets.
  • Bosphorus – Take a ferry or boat tour to enjoy views of both Europe and Asia.

For more information about attractions, culture, and local tips, please visit the following official tourism websites:

  • Visit Istanbul - The Istanbul Metropolitan Municipality

Taksim & Local Info

The conference venue is located near Taksim Square, one of Istanbul’s most central and vibrant areas. Taksim offers numerous hotels, restaurants, cafés, and easy access to public transportation.

  • Popular areas to stay: Taksim, Cihangir, Galata, Karaköy.
  • Metro, tram, and bus lines run frequently and are affordable.
  • Currency: Turkish Lira (₺). Credit cards are widely accepted.
  • Official language: Turkish. English is commonly understood in tourist areas.

How Can I Get to the Conference Venue?

The SPM 2026 conference will be held at Istanbul Technical University, Gümüşsuyu Campus, located close to Taksim Square. The venue is easily accessible by various means of transportation.

  • From Istanbul Airport (IST): Take the Havaist shuttle to Taksim (~40 min).
  • From Sabiha Gökçen Airport (SAW): Use Havabus to Taksim (~60–90 min).
  • Metro Access: Use M2 metro line to Taksim station, then walk ~10 minutes.
  • Taxi/Uber: Widely available; airport to venue approx. 30–60 min depending on traffic.
